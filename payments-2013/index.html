Marketplace

The main place to get apps.

No lock in

You are free to do payments any way you choose.

You do not have to use the Marketplace.

Countries

How to setup

  1. Make an awesome app
  2. Register your app on the marketplace

Create a payment account

So the payment provider (currently Bango) can pay you.

Price points

A price point represents one price across regions, currencies and carriers.

e.g: Tier 10, US $0.99, COP $2060.00, 3.99 zł, €0.89

https://developer.mozilla.org/en-US/docs/Web/Apps/Publishing/App_pricing

Choose price point

Paid apps only

In-app payments can use any price point

Then...

You will need to do a bit of work in your app.

Paid apps

After the payment is processed

Receipt is installed

https://wiki.mozilla.org/Apps/WebApplicationReceipt

Receipt is proof of payment

It is the app developers job to check receipts.

If you do not check receipts, your app can be installed for free.

Receipt verification

https://developer.mozilla.org/en-US/docs/Web/Apps/Publishing/Validating_a_receipt

Library: https://github.com/mozilla/receiptverifier

  1. Check crypto
  2. Check contents
  3. Verify against the marketplace

Receipt errors

Up to you:

  1. When to check receipts
  2. What to do when it fail

In app payments

Create some JSON that represents your payment in your app.

Call navigator.mozPay

https://developer.mozilla.org/en-US/docs/Web/API/window.navigator.mozPay

The payment flow will start.

Handling the response

We will post the response to you.

Your job to handle the response.
